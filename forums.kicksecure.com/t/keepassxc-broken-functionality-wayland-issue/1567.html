anon92632958:

On KeepassXC in the GUI the preview panel doesn’t show despite it being enabled?

It’s there, but needs to be expanded. If you look at the bottom of the window, you’ll see, right underneath the password table, a very small horizontal line. If you hover your mouse over that, it will turn into a “slider” icon. If you click and drag upward at that point, the preview pane should pop open. You have to drag up pretty far before it pops out.
