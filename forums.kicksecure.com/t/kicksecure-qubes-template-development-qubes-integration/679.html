In response to my [announcement of availability of Qubes Kicksecure Template for testers](https://forum.qubes-os.org/t/kicksecure-17-template/31893/11), @alimirjamali said: > Would it be possible to trigger a template build for r4.3 as well? For testing users who are on R4.3 alpha. ### Actual behavior Templates are built for Qubes stable version only. ### Additional information Why not simply always build for stable and development version of Qubes? I don't know if there are computational and/or storage restraints by Qubes build servers. related: * https://github.com/QubesOS/qubes-issues/issues/9573 ### Desired outcome of this ticket A Qubes core team member expressing the policy. Potential replies: * > No problem, it is OK and wanted to always build Templates for Qubes stable and Qubes development version. * > Qubes development Templates should be built on demand or when deemed necessary, but some sort of restraint would be desirable to save disk space. ### Potential outcome I'd modify [dm-qubes-templates-official-build-commands](https://github.com/Kicksecure/developer-meta-files/blob/master/usr/bin/dm-qubes-templates-official-build-commands) to add either a reminder or automation to build Templates also for Qubes development version.
