Hope I’m not too late to the party. In draft 1, the bottom-right logo looks like it says “Icksecure” due to the separation of the lock from the rest of the text; this is unfortunate given that “Ick” is associated with disgust in English. I’d suggest prepending the “K” letter there, similar to how the text in the bottom-left logo looks. This also applies to the similar-looking logos in draft 14, the top-right logo in draft 15, and the top logo in draft 18.
